My Projects

Building tools that connect, inform, and empower the Ipil community.

Ipil Today (App)

Ipil Today is a mobile application designed to keep the community informed and connected. It provides real-time national news, local updates, and community announcements.

  • • Real-time national news and local updates
  • • Highlights of local tourist spots and delicacies
  • • Access to emergency hotlines
  • • Easy-to-browse interface
  • • Lightweight and fast

Ipil Community Hub (Website)

The Ipil Community Hub website serves as the official platform to showcase and distribute the Ipil Today app. It provides app details, announcements, and recent updates.

  • • Central hub for app downloads and updates
  • • Announcements and news related to the app
  • • Project information and feature highlights
  • • Clean and user-friendly design
  • • Links to future community resources
